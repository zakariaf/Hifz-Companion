## Goal

The walking-skeleton spine becomes one tested end-to-end journey on the **real stack**: an `integration_test` that boots the wired `HifzApp` over a real on-disk WAL `AppDatabase` under a `FixedClock`, drives the minimal cold-start seed (E07-T06) so the redirect guard flips `appReady`, lands on Today (E07-T07/T08), reads a **real `Engine.buildToday`-selected** due page from the seeded card set, grades it one-tap through `CardRepository.recordReview` (the single write path), and asserts the `todayQueueProvider` Drift stream **re-emits** with the page rescheduled — all while a **throwing `HttpOverrides`** makes any stray socket a loud, named failure (proving the radio stays off). Every leg is addressed only by stable accessibility identifiers and asserted to pass the in-flow accessibility audit in each of `ar`/`fa`/`ckb` (RTL, locale numerals). This is the one apex journey of the four PRD make-or-break flows that this epic owns; it runs merge-blocking in CI on every PR and never queries a display string.

## Implementation notes

**TEST-FIRST (this task *is* a test — the spine journey is the deliverable).** Write the journey as failing assertions against the wired `HifzApp` first. The only production glue this task may force into existence is a **missing stable accessibility identifier** on a spine control (added in the owning feature target, never here) or a DEBUG-only seed/clock hook in the composition root if T06/T01 did not already expose one. No production behaviour beyond identifiers/hooks is authored.

1. **Tier & file** — `packages/app/integration_test/spine_journey_test.dart` (the `app` target is the only one that composes the full stack), opening with `IntegrationTestWidgetsFlutterBinding.ensureInitialized();` then `useOfflineTestPolicy();` (the shared throwing `HttpOverrides` from E07-T09 / `eng-write-dart-test` pattern 8). One named case, e.g. `seedThenGradeReschedulesOnToday`. This is the **fourth and last** apex `integration_test` of the epic — keep the journey suite to the four PRD flows (`eng-write-dart-test` pattern 1).

2. **Boot the real wired app, not a fake** — build the live collaborators exactly as `main()` does and supply them via `ProviderScope(overrides:)` around `const HifzApp()`: the on-disk `AppDatabase` over a unique temp dir (`Directory.systemTemp.createTempSync('hifz_spine_')`, same WAL `PRAGMA`s `main()` sets — `journal_mode=WAL`, `synchronous=FULL`; **never** `NativeDatabase.memory()` — this is the real stack, `eng-write-dart-test` pattern 9), a `FixedClock(CalendarDate.ymd(2026, 6, 16))` as `clockProvider`, the real `schedulerProvider`/`cardRepositoryProvider`/`activeProfileProvider`, and the **fake-ready-by-seed** `appReadyProvider` wiring from E07-T01 (the core pack is E05-verified; this journey does **not** download — it asserts the seed, not the network). Pump with `await tester.pumpWidget(...)`; advance with explicit `await tester.pump(duration)` between async legs — **never** `pumpAndSettle()` on an indefinite/calm indicator (`eng-write-dart-test` pattern 9 pitfalls).

3. **Leg 1 — land on onboarding via the redirect guard, not a push** — on first frame an un-ready device must already be at `/onboarding` (the guard, E07-T03/§6). Assert `find.bySemanticsIdentifier`/`find.byKey` for the `screen.onboarding` root resolves and the `/today` surface does **not** — proving the guard routed here structurally, with no imperative navigation. Do not call `router.go('/today')` to skip the seed.

4. **Leg 2 — drive the minimal cold-start seed (E07-T06)** — tap a small deterministic coverage selection (e.g. one held juz) by its stable coverage-cell identifier, pick **Solid/Shaky/Rusty** by the confidence-option identifier, and confirm. The seed's `commitPlacement` calls `Engine.coldStartCard(pageId, confidence, today)` per held page and persists the seeded cards through the **single write path** (one `db.transaction`, commit before republish). Address every control by identifier, never by the localized label (the per-locale re-run, note 9, depends on it).

5. **Leg 3 — the guard flips and Today renders a real engine-selected page** — after the seed commits, `appReady` is true and the guard resolves `/today`; pump until the `today.queue` list root appears. Assert at least one `today.row` / `today.page-card.<pageId>` exists and that the visible page is **selected by the real `Engine.buildToday`** over the seeded card set (Far → Near → New order) — i.e. the journey reads what the engine produced, it does not stub the queue. The page identity is one of the 604 muṣḥaf pages (C-031); the headline renders in the locale's digit block, FSI/PDI-isolated (note 9).

6. **Leg 4 — grade one page through the single write path** — tap the row's `grade.good` (or `grade.again`) control, which fires `TodayController.grade(pageId, Grade.good, source: ReviewSource.self)` → `CardRepository.recordReview(...)` with `errorLines == const []` (E07-T08). The grade routes through `recordReview` only — the journey opens no transaction, calls no DAO, and triggers no imperative refresh. Calm receipt only: assert **no** confetti/score/streak/badge/exclamation node exists in the subtree after the grade.

7. **Leg 5 — assert the queue stream re-emits with the page rescheduled** — pump until the committed transaction makes `todayQueueProvider` re-emit; assert the graded page reappears in its **new** track/position (or leaves its prior slot) and that its surfaced `dueAt` is the trust-clamped engine value (C-016) — the persist-before-republish loop observed end-to-end (`01` §4 step 6; `04` §3). The update must be the **consequence of the commit**, never an optimistic republish — so no `state = ...` before the await, asserted indirectly by the re-emit being the sole refresh path.

8. **(Optional) Leg 6 — literal process-kill confirmation** — T09 owns the deterministic headless disk-durability proof; this journey may add the **UI** confirmation that, after the grade, a relaunch over the same temp file shows the rescheduled page (re-boot a fresh `HifzApp` over the same `AppDatabase` file family, no graceful teardown). Keep it a thin confirmation — do **not** re-implement T09's `review_log`/D/S assertions here.

9. **Run the journey under each of `ar`/`fa`/`ckb`** — parameterize the case (or a `for (final locale in …)` loop) so the identical identifier-driven flow runs with `MaterialApp`'s locale forced to each of the three RTL locales. Because every `find` is by **accessibility identifier** (`screen.onboarding`, coverage cell, confidence option, `today.queue`, `today.row`, `today.page-card.<pageId>`, `grade.*`) and **never** by a display string, all three runs pass unchanged. Where a `Semantics` label is asserted, read it through `AppLocalizations.of(context)` for the active locale, never a hardcoded literal; the "Page N · Juz M" headline is verified to use the correct locale numeral block and stay isolated (note: this asserts the *render path* T07/T08 own, it does not author copy).

10. **In-flow accessibility audit** — at each end-state screen (onboarding seed, Today populated, Today after grade) call the `assertScreenPassesAudit(tester)` helper (the `accessibility_audit_guidelines` flow per `eng-write-dart-test`/the accessibility gate) inside the same journey, ignore-handler empty, so a contrast/label/tap-target regression on the spine is PR-blocking. Identifiers are test handles only — never user data, never localized text.

11. **DEBUG-only seed/clock hooks honored only in DEBUG** — if the journey needs a seed shortcut or the `FixedClock` is fed via a launch hook, it is wired through the injected `clockProvider`/seed provider in the composition root (E07-T01/T02), honored **only in DEBUG**, never in views and never in a release build. Prefer driving the real seed UI (Leg 2) over a backdoor; a backdoor exists only to make the clock deterministic.

12. **Pitfalls to avoid** — querying a **display string** instead of an accessibility identifier (the fa/ckb/ar re-run would then need edits — the cardinal sin here); using `NativeDatabase.memory()` instead of the real on-disk WAL store (this is the integration tier — `eng-write-dart-test` pattern 9); `pumpAndSettle()` on a calm/indefinite indicator (pump explicit durations); `router.go('/today')` / `Navigator.push` to **skip** the seed and bypass the redirect guard (the guard is the thing under test — `04` §6); any `DateTime.now()`/`Calendar.current` anywhere in the test (today is the `FixedClock`'s `CalendarDate`; E01's CI grep bans the wall clock); a **second** network client or any `http`/`dio`/`HttpClient` import (the throwing `HttpOverrides` must stay armed for the whole journey — C-048); optimistic republish or a `setState`/manual cache poke to "make" the queue update (the commit→stream re-emit is the only legal refresh); asserting a streak/score/badge/confetti exists (forbidden — and asserting its **absence** is required); rendering or asserting a muṣḥaf **glyph** (the reader is inert this epic); re-implementing T09's headless `review_log`/durability assertions; hardcoded left/right or physical-side `find` offsets instead of identifier-based finds; adding a graceful-shutdown crutch to production to make Leg 6 pass; duplicating the seed/queue/grade **unit** assertions T06/T07/T08 already own (this journey proves the **seam**, not the pieces).
